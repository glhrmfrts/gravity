Gravity Game

How to play: The goal is to get to the green planet without crashing in the red ones or the satellites, use the gravity of the red planets to curve your way down (or up) to the green planet.

Controls

  • Hold the mouse down to increase your launch power and release it to launch.
  • N: Start a random map (random positions).
  • R: Restart your position.
